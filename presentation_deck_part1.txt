### Slide 2: The Core Problem in AI Search
**Title:** The Shift from SEO to GEO
*   **The Paradigm Shift:** Users are moving away from traditional search engines (Google Search) to LLM-powered answer engines (ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity).
*   **The Blind Spot:** Brands currently have zero visibility into how often they are recommended by these LLMs, or what context they are mentioned in.
*   **The Opportunity:** Build a "Truth Layer" and analytics suite that allows brands to measure, track, and influence their share of voice within Generative AI responses.

---
### Slide 3: Feature Design - "LLM Share of Voice (SOV) Tracker"
**Title:** New Feature Design: LLM Share of Voice Tracker
*   **Objective:** Give brands a quantifiable metric for their visibility across top LLMs.
*   **How it Works:** 
    1.  User inputs target keywords (e.g., "Best CRM software").
    2.  The platform queries ChatGPT, Gemini, and Claude using automated testing.
    3.  It parses the outputs to see if the brand is mentioned, ranked, or hallucinated against.
*   **User Value:** Brands get a dashboard showing their "LLM Visibility Score" over time compared to competitors.

---
### Slide 4: Market Comparison
**Title:** Competitive Landscape
*   **Tool A (e.g., BrightEdge Generative Parser):** Focuses heavily on traditional SEO merging with Google's SGE, but lacks deep insights into standalone LLMs like ChatGPT.
*   **Tool B (e.g., Brandwatch):** Great for social listening, but does not simulate LLM queries or track AI-generated brand sentiment.
*   **Our GEO Platform Advantage:** We focus exclusively on the specific algorithms of LLMs. By providing actionable insights on how to format content to be "ingested" by AI (e.g., semantic density, structured data), we offer a unique value proposition.

### Slide 5: Short-Term Monetization Roadmap (Months 1-3)
**Title:** 3-Month Roadmap: Penetration & Validation
*   **Pricing Model:** Freemium Tier + Self-Serve Pro Tier ($99/mo).
*   **Target Audience:** Forward-thinking SEO agencies and tech-savvy marketing teams.
*   **Go-to-Market Strategy:** 
    *   Offer a free "One-Time Brand Audit" report (showing their current ChatGPT visibility).
    *   Upsell the Pro Tier for continuous monthly tracking and competitor comparison.
*   **Goal:** Reach 100 paying customers to validate product-market fit and cover API querying costs.

**Title:** 1-Year Roadmap: Enterprise Expansion
